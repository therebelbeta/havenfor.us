Best Man

RYAN BINGHAM

Hi, I'm Ryan Bingham. I grew up in Utah, moving between Salt Lake Valley and Cache Valley. These days I find myself in Chicago as I try to look upon God without dying! But really, I'm working on a master's degree in religious studies. In my spare time, I enjoy thinking through stories that help me develop the contours of my own philosophy/theology. I spend a lot of my study time working with Mormonism, especially Mormon scripture and Joseph Smith's theology.

Trent and I met as Mormon missionaries in Los Angeles. I connected with Trent's desire for authenticity and understanding. When we were both back in Utah, these same traits fortified our friendship, and we became partners in leisure and thought. Trent continues to provide a haven for me, one that allows me to take delight in my growth. When I first met Emily, I was struck by the fact that she did the same. I'm very excited for the big day.
