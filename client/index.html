A Haven For Us

Haven: A place of safety or sanctuary. A place of refuge and rest. A place where we can truly be who and what we want to be.
We have strived to be a haven for each other, and invite you to join us in celebration of our Haven when we solemnize our relationship amid the brilliant red stone of Parley's Canyon.

Where and When

The ceremony will be held on Wednesday, August 5, 2015 at Louland Falls, Utah. It will start at Six O'Clock in the evening. A celebration with dinner and dancing will follow at the same location. Check the map to get the direction. We have arranged accommodations at a nearby comfort inn for guests who are making a bit of a trip. Check for accomodations.
